Ratan Tata

Indian Industrialist & Philanthropist

1937 – 2024

About

Ratan Tata was one of India's most respected business leaders, known for transforming the Tata Group into a globally recognized conglomerate.

Under his leadership, the group expanded internationally, acquiring global brands and strengthening India’s presence in the global market.

Beyond business, he was admired for his humility, ethics, and philanthropic contributions in education, healthcare, and rural development.

Major Contributions

Global Expansion

Expanded Tata Group internationally with major acquisitions.

Innovation Leadership

Encouraged innovation across automobiles, steel, and technology.

Philanthropy

Supported large-scale initiatives in education and healthcare.
